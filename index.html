The Arts and Music in Prisons Project

Harmony Beyond Bars:
Inspiring Change Through Art and Music

View Our Mission

We Want to Rethink Corrections

Our mission is to provide comprehensive and accessible art and music education programs within prisons. By fostering a safe and inclusive environment, we aim to inspire personal growth, emotional healing, and social development among the incarcerated population. At the same time, we aim to decrease recidivism by connecting communities and empowering individuals with foundations for success.

How It Works

1. We create a network of volunteer educators who want to give back to the community.

2. We establish creative in-prison programs that potential students can register for.

3. Our board reviews every student registration and arranges small student/educator pods.

4. Students who remain in good standing learn skills in art, music, or writing fields.

5. Students may submit their creative works to be displayed on our site and available for purchase.

A portion of the proceeds from each sale are deposited into commissary accounts, with the remaining being used to continue funding our program.

Goals

The AMP Project aims to:

  • Decrease violence in prisons by helping individuals find meaningful work.
  • Establishing incentives for individuals to stay in good standing during incarceration.
  • Help individuals create a source of income to cover the costs of incarceration.
  • Establish programs that help individuals develop skills that can create bonds and outlets.
  • Decrease recidivism by creating means by which individuals can succeed in the world post-incarceration.
